UTK.io

Built a social network for mobile Minecraft players. Users can use the app to post their own in-game content (maps, skins, ...), chat with their friends or post status updates, similar to other social networks like Instagram or Twitter.

Built the whole platform as the sole developer from scratch, including the native Android app, backend and frontend. Managed marketing, social media channels, distribution, monetization and growth of the userbase.

Started developing the platform in summer 2014, with the first release being in late 2014. Oversaw the platforms operation, improved and extended it continuously based on customer feedback for almost 5 years until selling it successfully in 2019 to focus on other ventures.

The app is available on the Google Play Store.
